AI Detection

State of the art machine learning model trained to detect whether media is human-generated or AI-generated. Currently, Catch.ai's machine learning model is 99.7% accurate in determining whether text is AI-generated.

Dataset

The model is trained on over a million examples of human-generated text and AI-generated text.

The data includes human sources such as news articles, Wikipedia pages, and social media posts. AI-generated text examples are sourced from various state of the art text foundation models.

Why it matters

With the advent of exponentially improving generative AI models, we deserve to know whether we are interacting with AI. Generative models can now be used to proliferate false information or impersonate humans.
